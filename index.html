LO Maul Attack

Roles & Responsibilities
⬆ SCREW

The Jumper. Triple extension on landing. Low and spread feet. Establish the anchor of the maul.

🎯 POINTERS

The Lifters. Slightly turn on the way down. Stay engaged, protect and attack forward — Point / Spear.

🏉 BALLERS

+1 or first receiver. Gets the ball — set the height right to receive cleanly and distribute.

💪 DRIVERS

Shoulders to Bums. Protect the Baller and add positive support to Pointers. If under pressure, Baller calls BACK.

🔄 BACKERS

Fill space first. Effect change. Channel the ball to the back of the maul.

Forward Covering 9 A forward takes the scrum-half role to ensure full defensive coverage across the lineout.

Scrum Principles

Foundations & Timing
⬇

Lower Position

100% of our scrums — we are in the lower position. No exceptions. Dominate the height battle.

🏉

Own Ball — In & Out

Clean strike on 2, not on 9's choice. Fast, decisive. Ball in, ball out on our terms.

💥

Defensive Fight

Stay in the fight defensively. Collective drive on strike through TightHead.

Scrum Sequence — Crouch → Bind → Set
CROUCH
Chest movement low — feet do NOT move. Hooker sets first (brake foot and LOW height). Foot out so we do not need to go head to head.
BIND
Hands on bind. All three front-rowers adjust feet. Hooker still up. Props bind simultaneously (Tight head slightly forward — 3-2-1 hit).
SET
Hooker fires foot back. No movement / maintain pressure until happy. Forward and down movement — engage and move opposition feet. Hooker hooks when ready, NOT on 9. Triangle hook, 9 onto the boot. Outside shoulder inline with hooker ear (Scrum Half).
