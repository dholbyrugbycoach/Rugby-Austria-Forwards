LO Maul Attack

Roles & Responsibilities
🎯 TARGET

The Jumper. Triple extension on landing. Low and spread feet. Establish the anchor of the maul.

⬆️ POINTERS

The Lifters. Slightly turn on the way down. Stay engaged, protect and attack forward — Point / Spear.

🏉 BALLERS

+1 or first receiver. Gets the ball — set the height right to receive cleanly and distribute.

🔩 SCREWS

Shoulders to Bums. Protect the Baller and add positive support to Pointers. Screws shoulder on to bums or into gaps to create distance between opposition.

⚙️ BACKERS

Fill space first. Effect change. Anticipate changes of direction, bodies and breakaway.

★ Important Updates / Small Details - Big Impact
Attacking Maul — Screws closer to the touchline head inside, further from the touchline head out.
* Hooker becomes a Screw when throwing to the front of the LO instead of a Backer, for speed and safety.

Lineout Defence

5-Man & 7-Man Opposition
● Blue — Lifters and Look for driving positions ● Green — Strong / compete / Jump ● Red — Lifter / DSpace / Hinge
Standard Defensive Shape 7-MAN
If we set early, be prepared that they may choose to attack the dead space between the pods. Standard shape but could change sporadically and delayed.
Dynamic Hinge Option 7-MAN · COMPLEX
Hinge defence on E to target 2–4 space. This is more dynamic but complex — requires Hinge Player and jumper to be as explosive as possible. Pre-call and awareness if we move to this option.
9 — 5m Channel 9 in the 5m channel to allow extra forward into the LO. Use often if you see a PLUS1 or Strike play heavy at the hinge with context of play.
Normal Positioning 9 to sweep, keep the inside or connect the hinge. Tail of the LO to support.
Protect Hinge / Target Identify the target jumper. Protect the hinge and cover any bad ball or overthrown ball.
One Goal — All In Defence LO has one clear objective. Everyone committed to that goal. Attack the floor or in air.

Scrum Principles

Foundations & Timing
⬇

Lower Position

100% of our scrums — we are in the lower position. No exceptions. Dominate the height battle.

🏉

Own Ball — In & Out

Clean strike on 2, not on 9's choice. Fast, decisive. Ball in, ball out on our terms.

💥

Defensive Fight

Stay in the fight defensively. Collective drive on strike through TightHead.

Scrum Sequence — Crouch → Bind → Set
CROUCH
Chest movement low — feet do NOT move. Hooker sets first (brake foot and LOW height). Foot out so we do not need to go head to head.
BIND
Hands on bind. All three front-rowers adjust feet. Hooker still up. Props bind simultaneously (Tight head slightly forward — 3-2-1 hit).
SET
Hooker fires foot back. No movement / maintain pressure until happy. Forward and down movement — engage and move opposition feet. Hooker hooks when ready, NOT on 9. Triangle hook, 9 onto the boot. Outside shoulder inline with hooker ear (Scrum Half).
